Are you...

  • Experienced with grant writing?
  • Experienced with crowdfunding?
  • Experienced with handling nonprofit finances?
  • A self-starter who works well both collaboratively and individually?

Want to...

Want to...

  • Draft and oversee content going on the Uplift accounts?

Emerging Social Networks Researcher [Volunteer]

  • Research oriented?

  • Research and document trends in the emerging online communities at the creator, platform, and fan level?
  • Identify online communities that are beginning to bloom, particularly among teens?
