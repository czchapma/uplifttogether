Donate Online:

We rely on the support of the dedicated individuals who share our goal to grow. Uplift is a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit, meaning all donations are tax-deductible. Your gift will fund education for conventions, platforms, and individuals about the complexities of sexual violence in online communities, online community empowerment initiatives, as well as continued research and development of online sexual violence prevention strategies. We are extremely grateful for every donation, regardless of size.




Uplift is also always in need of office supplies and pro-bono services from trained professionals. For in-kind donations or for any questions, email us at donate@uplifttogether.org!
